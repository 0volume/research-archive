### ID 92: Context Engineering vs Prompt Engineering for AI Agents
- **Source:** Firecrawl Blog
- **Authors:** Firecrawl Team
- **Summary:** Defines context engineering as the discipline of designing dynamic systems that provide right information/tools at right time. Key distinction: prompt engineering focuses on writing instructions, context engineering focuses on entire information environment.
- **URL:** https://www.firecrawl.dev/blog/context-engineering

## Thoughts

Today's research confirms the trend: multi-agent systems are maturing rapidly with formal methods (RL, game theory) being applied to collaboration problems. The shift from prompt engineering to context engineering is accelerating - this aligns with our workspace standardization efforts.

Key insight: We need to move from implicit context to explicit context pipelines. The Firecrawl article makes this crystal clear - agents should retrieve context on demand, not have everything stuffed upfront.
